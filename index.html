ABOUT DADACOIN

Most popular memecoins rely on bright colors, cute animals, and a cheerful atmosphere. As a result, they all end up looking the same — the same style, the same energy, the same empty promises.

DADACOIN will not follow this formula. Instead, it will take a radically different approach. Its mascot is a grumpy panda, tired of market illusions and amused by the surrounding naivety — a character who finds nearly everything ridiculous, especially other memecoins he sees as simple clones.

DADACOIN will embody an original ideology: a satire, a caricature of the crypto world, built through dark humor and the sharp personality of its mascot. It is a parody of the market, a universe that has become too euphoric, too noisy, too "woof-woof."

Roadmap

Phase 1 – Launch & Expansion

Release of the official website and community channels (Telegram, X)

Publication of the community guide

Community building (organization and moderation)

Listing on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap

Phase 2 – Growth & Visibility

Release and diffusion of DADACOIN memes

Expansion of a large community

Deployment of the token on PancakeSwap

Phase 3 – Recognition & Partnerships

Listings on major CEX platforms

Partnerships with artists, Web3 projects, and communities

Phase 4 – Community PvP Game

Creation of an online game where players form a team of six monsters and battle in turn-based duels

Both players wager DADA tokens, and the winner takes the entire pot

The game's development will be funded by the development reserve
