2. Vision and Mission

Most popular memecoins rely on bright colors, cute animals, and a cheerful atmosphere. As a result, they all end up looking the same — the same style, the same energy, the same empty promises.

The foundation of a project like this lies in its philosophy.

DADACOIN will not follow this formula.

Instead, it will take a radically different approach.

Its mascot is a grumpy panda, tired of market illusions and amused by the surrounding naivety — a character who finds nearly everything ridiculous, especially other memecoins he sees as simple clones.

DADACOIN will embody an original ideology:

a satire, a caricature of the crypto world, built through dark humor and the sharp personality of its mascot.

It is a parody of the market, a universe that has become too euphoric, too noisy, too "woof-woof."

3. Objective

Create a community-driven memecoin that goes against current standards.

Decentralize DADACOIN by maximizing supply dilution.

5. Security and Governance

Governance

DADACOIN will operate without a central authority.

All decisions will be driven by the community.

The contract ownership will be renounced to guarantee full decentralization.

Security

The smart contract will undergo two independent security audits.

Its source code will be public, readable on BscScan and GitHub.
